Happy hamster

Once upon a time, golden hamster and gray hamster lived in a quiet rural village.

However, the Golden Hamster loved eating so much that it grew tremendously.

The surprised gray hamster took the Golden Hamster to the hospital.

The doctor hamster, who saw the Golden Hamster, pondered for a while and beat the Golden Hamster's chubby belly, saying, "Argh!"

When doctor Hamster beat the Golden Hamster's belly, the Golden Hamster farted.

The Golden Hamster has returned to its original size. The reason the size of the Golden Hamster grew was because of the gas filled in the stomach.

The two hamsters said, "Thank you, doctor!" and headed home.
